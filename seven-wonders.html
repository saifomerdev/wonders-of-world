The Seven Wonders of the World

Explore the magnificent structures that have captivated humanity for centuries

Great Wall of China

An ancient series of fortifications stretching over 13,000 miles across northern China.

Petra

An archaeological wonder carved into rose-red cliffs in the Jordanian desert.

Christ the Redeemer

An iconic Art Deco statue overlooking Rio de Janeiro from Mount Corcovado.

Machu Picchu

A 15th-century Inca citadel set high in the Andes Mountains of Peru.

Chichen Itza

A complex of Mayan ruins featuring the iconic El Castillo pyramid in Mexico.

Colosseum

An ancient amphitheater in Rome, the largest ever built during the Roman Empire.

Taj Mahal

An ivory-white marble mausoleum in Agra, India, symbolizing eternal love.
